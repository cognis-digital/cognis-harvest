# Methods & Schemas

## Schemas

**Scene:**
```json
{"id": str, "pixel_area_ha": float, "width": int, "height": int,
 "bands": ["blue","green","red","nir","swir1","sar"],
 "geotransform": {"origin_lat": .., "origin_lon": .., "dlat": .., "dlon": ..},
 "pixels": [{"row": int, "col": int, "values": {band: float}, "cloud": bool}]}
```

**Training samples:** `[{"class": str, "values": {band: float}}]`

**Coefficients:** `{"climate_factor": f, "soil_factor": f, "crops": {class: {"yield_kg_per_ha": f, "price_per_kg": f}}}`

## Methods

- **Spectral indices.** NDVI `(nir−red)/(nir+red)`, SAVI, NDWI, NBR.
- **Classification.** Per-class centroids from training samples; a pixel is
  assigned the minimum-Euclidean-distance class over the *available* bands, with
  a softmax confidence. Cloud-flagged pixels drop optical bands and classify on
  SAR only (lower confidence).
- **Area.** `area_ha = crop_pixels × pixel_area_ha`; a confidence interval is
  `area × (1 − accuracy)` when accuracy is supplied.
- **Yield.** `production = area × yield_per_ha × climate × soil`;
  `value = production × price`. Coefficients are illustrative.
- **Change.** Classify two scenes of the same grid; a pixel is *new cultivation*
  if crop at t2 but not at t1 (and *removed* for the reverse).

## Evaluation
`bench/` plants known crop parcels, then measures classification accuracy/F1,
area MAPE, cloud-vs-clear degradation, and change-detection P/R/F1 against that
ground truth. All numbers in `RESULTS.md` are regenerated by `bench/run_all.py`.
